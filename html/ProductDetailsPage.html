Trex

Description

This is a hand drawn illustration of a Trex. It is from the poker card set jurassic Downfall.

Application instructions

To apply the sticker in a good manner, you first have to find the place where you want to put it. Then remove the backing of the sticker and place it carefully on the selected place. After that you should press well on it for a few seconds and squeeze the remaining air out of it. Now you are all set!
